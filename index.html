FAQ

What is the HackDFW Fellowship?

The HackDFW fellowship is a program designed to recruit some of the best tech talent to the top companies in Dallas. We are looking for talented graduates and undergraduates to intern in Dallas over summer 2017 in a cohesive program. You are given free housing and weekly social events and dinners, where you will meet some of the most influential people in Dallas, all while working at some of Dallasâ€™s best companies. We provide as many opportunities as we can for our Fellows to be successful and make their mark on Dallas.

When do I apply?

You can apply beginning now. We will start reviewing resumes in mid-November, and begin interviews in December/January for the first round. Applications close at the end of December.

How do I apply?

You can apply online at the link above. We require a resume, a code sample, a link to projects (at hackathons or your own side projects), and a brief personal statement about your goals and desire to join this program.

What is the application process?

First, submit your application. We review each application using a holistic process, and all elements of your application will be considered. We then reach out to the top candidates and conduct interviews. There will be a behavioral interview and a technical interview. After that, should you progress, we will begin match interviews. These interviews will be in Dallas and directly with the company you have been matched to. If they accept you, you will be placed with that company - if not, you will be matched again. You can match up to three times.

To recap:

  • Apply online.
  • Get a behavioral and technical interview if selected.
  • Interview in Dallas directly with companies
  • If selected, congratulations!
  • If not, match up to two more times.

What companies are participating?

We are currently filling out our inaugural hosting companies class. We expect to release the list beginning in December - expect many very cool Dallas companies, from large well-known household names to small startups working hard on intriguing problems.

What else should I know about the fellowship?

Expect to be challenged every day. This is a prestigious program, and you will be tested accordingly - but we also aim for you to have a lot of fun with us, from weekly social events to the housing we provide, as well as the amazing opportunities to participate and network with Dallas-area tech executives and influencers.
